# üöö Driver Workflow: Acceptance to Completion

## Overview
This document outlines the complete workflow for drivers, from finding new jobs to completing them.

## üìã Finding & Accepting Jobs

1.  **View Available Jobs**
    *   Navigate to the **Driver Dashboard**.
    *   Scroll down to the **"Job Queue"** section.
    *   Here you will see all **Pending** bookings that have not yet been assigned to a driver.

2.  **Accept a Job**
    *   Click the **"Accept"** button on a job card.
    *   Confirm the action.
    *   The job will officially start, and it will trigger an SMS to the customer: _"Driver X is on the way (ETA: 30 mins)"_.
    *   The job moves to the **"Current Job"** section at the top of the dashboard.

## üìç Performing the Job

3.  **Navigation & Contact**
    *   Use the **Route Map** to see the customer's location.
    *   Click **"Directions"** to open Google Maps navigation.
    *   Use **"Call"** or **"Message"** to contact the customer.

4.  **Job Status Updates**
    *   **Start Job**: When you begin the journey.
    *   **Arrived**: When you reach the location.

## ‚úÖ Completing the Job

5.  **Completion & Payment**
    *   Once the work is done, click **"Complete"**.
    *   Confirm the completion.
    *   **Invoice Generated**: This automatically creates an invoice for the customer.
    *   **Customer Notification**: The customer receives an SMS to make a payment.

## üêû Troubleshooting
*   **"No Active Jobs"**: Means you haven't accepted a job yet. Check the Job Queue below.
*   **"No new jobs available"**: There are currently no pending bookings from customers.
*   **Map not loading**: Ensure the customer provided a valid location pin during booking.
